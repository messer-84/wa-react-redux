IN THE SPOTLIGHT
RESULTS - a Sphere Company Value

 

 

It’s about realizing the goal, making a difference and seeing the end result. This is why our innovation and self-directed behaviors aren’t in “left field” … they are aligned – towards a result. Common vision.

 
 

 

To achieve intended results in our work, we collectively must:

  • Help our colleagues be their best by providing coaching and constructive feedback
  • Work with others as a team to accomplish results and win
  • Have a “can do” attitude and drive to get the job done
  • Think “outside the box”
  • Make people feel valued and appreciated
  • Celebrate results!

 
 
 

 

Learn more about the different types of organizational values and why they matter here: https://hbr.org/2002/07/make-your-values-mean-something
